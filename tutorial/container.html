What can I do with container?

Container allows you to add assets in a list in order to organize them.

We will use 5 gifs to show you how to use container:

  • How to add assets into the container
  • How to resize the container
  • How to add title and background into the container
  • How to grow/shrink the container
  • How to margin the assets in container

How to add assets into the container

  1. Select Other tool and then select Container.
  2. Drag the assets from the board into the container.

In the illustration below, we added a sticky note and a picture as assets into the container.

How to resize the container

  1. Select the container
  2. Click the square on the right-bottom of the container.
  3. It will change to the four-arrows icon,and then you can change the height and width of the container.

In the illustration below, we changed the assets from vertical to horizontal by resizing the container.

How to add title and background into the container

  1. Click container and then click three dots on the bottom of the container.
  2. In the three dots, there is a container settings icon on the left.
  3. After you click it, you will see a container settings window pops up.
  4. Add/change the title and background color to the one you like and then click Save.
  5. Now, you have a container with title and background color.

In the illustration below, we added title and background color for the container.

How to grow/shrink the container

  1. Click container and then click three dots on the bottom of the container.
  2. In the three dots, there is a container settings icon on the left.
  3. After you click it, you will see a container settings window pops up.
  4. If you want to auto grow/shrink the container when you add new assets in it, leave a checkmark on Auto grow/shrink. If you don't want it, deselect it.

In the illustration below, we showed you the difference between keep auto grow/shrink and deselect it.

How to margin the assets in container

  1. Click container and then click three dots on the bottom of the container.
  2. In the three dots, there is a container settings icon on the left.
  3. After you click it, you will see a container settings window pops up.
  4. Change the margin in order to have a perfect distance between assets and container.

In the illustration below, we changed the margin from 10 to 15.
